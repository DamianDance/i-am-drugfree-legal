I Am DrugFree Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Overview

I Am DrugFree: Sober Tracker is a private self-tracking app for recovery routines. The app is designed around local storage. It does not require an account, does not include ads, does not include analytics SDKs, and does not use cloud sync for the core tracker.

Data stored on your device

The app may store recovery data locally on your device, including your recovery focus, quit date and time, personal reasons, daily cost and time estimates, reminder settings, optional privacy lock setting, optional profile image, daily check-ins, mood, craving level, trigger tags, urge logs, journal entries, relapse or reset history, rewards, saved money, and saved time.

Local storage

Recovery data is stored locally using Apple platform storage such as SwiftData and AppStorage. The developer does not receive this recovery data from the app as part of normal use.

No account, analytics, ads, or tracking

The app does not create user accounts, does not use third-party advertising, does not use analytics SDKs, and does not track you across apps or websites.

Reminders and privacy lock

If you enable reminders, iOS stores and presents local notifications on your device. If you enable Face ID or passcode protection, authentication is handled by the system. The app does not receive or store your biometric data.

Export and sharing

If you choose to export or share your recovery data, the exported text is passed to the iOS share sheet at your request. The destination app, service, or person you choose may process that information according to its own privacy practices.

Support resource links

The app includes links to external support resources such as 988 Lifeline, SAMHSA, FindTreatment.gov, Alcoholics Anonymous, Narcotics Anonymous, Al-Anon Family Groups, Gamblers Anonymous, the National Council on Problem Gambling, Smokefree.gov, and CDC quit smoking resources. Opening those links may send your browser to a third-party website. Those websites are operated by their own providers and have their own privacy practices.

Data deletion

You can delete local recovery data from inside the app where the deletion option is provided, or by deleting the app from your device. Deleting the app removes the app's local data from that device according to iOS behavior.

Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Medical and emergency disclaimer

I Am DrugFree is a self-tracking and support tool. It is not medical care, detox support, therapy, crisis care, or emergency help. If you are in danger, in withdrawal, or at risk of harming yourself, contact local emergency services or a qualified professional immediately.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the app changes or when legal requirements change. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
