Common help topics

  • Update your recovery focus, quit date, reasons, cost, time estimates, and reminders in app settings.
  • Use the Today tab for clean-day progress, pledge, review, urge log, journal, and reset notes.
  • Use the Toolkit tab for coping cards, an urge wave timer, grounding steps, and support resource links.
  • Use export if you want to keep a local copy or share data with someone you trust.
  • Delete local data from the app where available, or delete the app from your device.

Important safety note

I Am DrugFree is not emergency help, medical care, detox support, or therapy. If you are in immediate danger, in withdrawal, or at risk of harming yourself, contact local emergency services or a qualified professional immediately.
